Album Title:

Our project back in 2004 was Leuthen. We felt that a snow battle in 15mm was something rarely seen at shows, so we thought this would stand out. From the reaction at the shows it seemed we were right.

 

The rules used were Age of Reason. To give the units depth we doubled up the base sizes.

The figures used were all Old Glory 15. Our link page will take you to Battle Honours UK, who supply these.
