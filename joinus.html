Information for New Starters

 

We are a friendly club with a diverse membership who come from all walks of life. As a club we meet every two weeks on Sunday evenings at Earlswood Village hall. 

As you can see from the pictures on our site we enjoy many periods of warfare and a variety of scales. 

We are always looking for like minded new members who enjoy playing wargames simply for the pleasure that playing with good company gives rather than needing to win every game. That's not to say that we don't like winning! 

The club has been going for many years and we have built up quite a collection of terrain which is regularly used at the club meetings. 

If you are new to the hobby, please do not worry. Come along, try out a few games. See what takes your fancy and then start building your own army. There is lots of experience in the club and we can help you get started. 

Also - first night is free for all new members. After that, it is only £4 per meeting or you can pay a one off subscription of £40 and that pays for all your clubs for that year. 

Interested? Drop us an email and we can get you involved at the next club meeting.
