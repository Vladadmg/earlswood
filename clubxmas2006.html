Christmas 2006

 

Every year we run an all day game over the Christmas break. It's a chance to put on one or two really big games and also show off our new Christmas jumpers!

This year we ran four games.

A refight of the first Newbury battle (25mm ECW).

A Skirmish based on the action around the fight Hougomont at Waterloo (25mm Napoleonic).

A Renaissance Italian Wars (25mm).

A 6mm Napoleonic battle.
